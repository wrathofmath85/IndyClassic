Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

Indy Classic 2026 (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide when registering for the tournament.

Information We Collect

When you register for the tournament, we may collect information including:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Any additional information you provide during registration

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Register you for the tournament
  • Send tournament-related communications via SMS and/or email
  • Provide event details, scheduling updates, announcements, and important notifications
  • Communicate information relevant to your participation in Indy Classic 2026

SMS Communications

By providing your phone number during registration and opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages related to Indy Classic 2026. These messages may include a welcome letter, tournament updates, day-one recap information, announcements such as when lunch has arrived, and a thank-you message at the conclusion of the tournament.

  • Message frequency varies, but users can generally expect approximately 6–10 messages during tournament week
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • You may opt out at any time by replying STOP
  • For help, reply HELP

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

We may only use or disclose your information as needed to:

  • Operate and administer the tournament
  • Provide tournament communications you requested
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Rights

To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, contact us at IndyTennisclassic@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date when applicable.

SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

By registering for Indy Classic 2026 and opting in to SMS messaging, you agree to the following terms and conditions related to tournament text communications.

SMS Messaging Service

Users who opt in may receive tournament-related SMS messages from Indy Classic 2026, including:

  • Welcome information and event details
  • Tournament updates
  • Schedule notifications
  • Announcements, including when lunch has arrived
  • Recap messages or event wrap-ups
  • A final thank-you message at tournament conclusion

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies, but users can generally expect approximately 6–10 messages over the course of tournament week.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP.

End of Messaging

Tournament-related messaging will end after the conclusion of Indy Classic 2026. Users will not receive additional messages after the event unless they separately opt in again for a future tournament or communication program.

Liability

Indy Classic 2026 is not responsible for delayed or undelivered messages caused by carrier issues, technical failures, or circumstances outside our reasonable control.

Changes to These Terms

We may update these Terms & Conditions from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact Information

For questions regarding these Terms & Conditions, contact us at IndyTennisclassic@gmail.com.
