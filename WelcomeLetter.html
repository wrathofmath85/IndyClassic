Tournament Details

WARM-UP
Limited to 5 minutes—please help us stay on schedule by respecting this rule.

STAYING ON SCHEDULE
If you see a handshake on court, text the tournament desk: (708) 542-5972.

RAFFLES
Tickets will be sold at the courts and banquet.
Prizes include free tournament entries and more!
Winners drawn at the banquet, but most prizes can be claimed virtually or Monday at the courts.

MATCH FORMAT
Main Draw: Best 2 of 3 sets; 10-point tiebreaker in lieu of 3rd set.
Consolation (except finals): One pro set; 7-point tiebreak at 7-all.

CODE OF CONDUCT
The GLTA has seen an increase in incidents lately—let’s keep this tournament fun and respectful.
Disrespect toward players, staff, or club employees will not be tolerated and will result in automatic disqualification.
Line judges are available upon request—stop the match and alert the desk if needed.

MATCH READINESS
Stay near the tournament desk as your match time approaches.
It is your responsibility to be ready.
GLTA lateness penalties:

  • Up to 5 minutes: 1 game
  • 5–10 minutes: 2 games
  • 10–15 minutes: 3 games
  • Over 15 minutes: match forfeit
“Early is on time, on time is late, and late is DEAD!”

We’re looking forward to a weekend of tennis, fun, and friendships!
