A Note to Players

On behalf of the IndyTennis Board of Directors, Brady Wagenknecht, Mark Phillips, and myself—welcome to Indianapolis and the 2026 Indy Classic - Presented by CH Douglas & Gray Wealth Management!

We want this weekend to be as enjoyable as possible, so please don’t hesitate to reach out to Brady, Mark, or me with any questions.

Draws and match times will be posted Tuesday evening, and I’ll send a follow-up email once they’re available. If you would like to change your division or request a doubles partner, please email me before Sunday evening.

If you are looking to practice before the tournament, IRC has waived their guest fee. Just mention you are playing in the GLTA tournament this weekend when booking a court. If you are very serious about your practice, we are using Wilson balls for our tournament.

Tournament Details

Warm-Up

Limited to 5 minutes—please help us stay on schedule by respecting this rule.

Staying on Schedule

If you see a handshake on court, text the tournament desk: (708) 542-5972.

Raffles

Tickets will be sold at the courts and banquet.

Prizes include free tournament entries and more.

Winners are drawn at the banquet, but most prizes can be claimed virtually or Monday at the courts.

Match Format

  • Main Draw: Best 2 of 3 sets; 10-point tiebreaker in lieu of 3rd set
  • Consolation (except finals): One pro set; 7-point tiebreak at 7-all

Code of Conduct

The GLTA has seen an increase in incidents lately—let’s keep this tournament fun and respectful.

Disrespect toward players, staff, or club employees will not be tolerated and will result in automatic disqualification.

Line judges are available upon request—stop the match and alert the desk if needed.

Match Readiness

Stay near the tournament desk as your match time approaches. It is your responsibility to be ready.

  • Up to 5 minutes late: 1 game
  • 5–10 minutes late: 2 games
  • 10–15 minutes late: 3 games
  • Over 15 minutes late: match forfeit
“Early is on time, on time is late, and late is DEAD!”
